Lipa na Mpesa Procedure




1. Select "Pay Bill" From Mpesa menu.
2. Enter Gizani Business Number ie. 972988
3. Enter Account Details
4. Enter the exact amount that you would like to pay for your case
5. Enter your Mpesa Bill
6. Confirm your Details complete the payment.

7. You will receive a confirmation sms.
8. Enter the transaction number on the provided input field and click enter.
